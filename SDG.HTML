What is SDG 17?

SDG 17 aims to enhance global partnerships to support the implementation of the 2030 Agenda for Sustainable Development. It emphasizes collaboration between governments, private sector, and civil society to mobilize resources, share knowledge, and build capacity.

Key Targets

Finance

Mobilize additional financial resources for developing countries to implement the SDGs.

Technology

Enhance North-South, South-South, and triangular regional and international cooperation on science, technology, and innovation.

Capacity Building

Enhance international support for implementing effective and targeted capacity-building in developing countries.

Trade

Increase the exports of developing countries and implement a universal, rules-based, open, non-discriminatory, and equitable multilateral trading system.

How You Can Help
